    About Al Mehfuz Khanqah

    Al Mehfuz Khanqah ae Qadriyaa was established as a center for spiritual cultivation, learning, and the transmission of sacred knowledge within the noble Qadri tradition ‚Äî a lineage tracing back through generations of blessed scholars to the great Shaykh Abdul Qadir al-Jilani (may Allah sanctify his secret).

    Our khanqah welcomes sincere seekers from all walks of life who wish to draw closer to the Divine through prayer, remembrance, and the company of the righteous. We uphold the classical tradition of tasawwuf ‚Äî inner purification and spiritual excellence ‚Äî guided by qualified teachers in an environment of peace and brotherhood.
